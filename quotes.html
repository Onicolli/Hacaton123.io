Quotes

"I don't believe in God, but I think he's looking at me."

season 5 episode 2

The situation in which the Professor said this phrase is due to the fact that he and his team were in a critical situation where the police were closing in on them and the situation was becoming increasingly tense. The phrase expresses his feelings of helplessness and hope for salvation at a critical moment, despite his skepticism towards religion.

“Sometimes you have to sacrifice a few to save many.”

season 4 episode 9

The situation in which the Professor uttered this phrase is concerned about the critical stage of the grandiose takeover of the Bank of Spain (La Casa de Moneda) and his attempt to organize a rescue operation for his team, which takes place inside the bank. This quote gives him the determination and willingness to make sacrifices to prevent everyone involved in the conquest.

"Words are the most powerful weapon: they can kill, save and change the world."

season 3 episode 9

The situation in which the Professor uttered this phrase is related to the development and execution of his complex plan to rob the Golden Mint in Spain. The phrase emphasizes the importance of psychological tactics and the use of words and deception to achieve goals.

"War only begins when the government drops its mask and takes up arms."

season 2 episode 1

The situation in which the Professor uttered this phrase is related to the continuation of his plan to rob the Spanish National Monetary Treasury. The phrase highlights his view of the conflict between a group of robbers and the government as a war that begins on both sides.

These are not all the professor’s phrases; more quotes will not come out soon))
