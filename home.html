Plot

The plot of "Money Heist" unfolds in five gripping acts, each representing an exciting chapter in the heist's story. At the outset, the charismatic Professor assembles a team of eight criminals, each hiding behind a city name and a Salvador Dali mask. After seizing the Royal Mint, they start printing counterfeit money. Following the initial success, tension brews inside the Mint, and confrontations with the police intensify. Season 4 delves into negotiation strategies and character dynamics. Season 5 introduces new adversaries and far-reaching consequences. The series' conclusion brings dramatic twists, altering the fates of its characters and forcing them into decisive actions. "Money Heist" is a tale of crime, intrigue, and sacrifices interwoven in an exhilarating struggle for survival and freedom.

Description

Life Story: Professor was born in Russia into a family of a renowned scientist and university professor. He was a gifted student and always showed a keen interest in criminal investigations. His intelligence and education led him to become a university professor and an expert in planning complex criminal operations.

Plan: Professor devised a detailed and cunning plan to rob the National Mint of Spain, which became the central plot of the series. He assembled a team of eight criminals, each of whom wore a mask with the image of Salvador Dali's face and adopted the name of a city like Tokyo, Berlin, Rio, and so on. Under his guidance, the team seized the Mint and began the process of printing counterfeit money for several months.
